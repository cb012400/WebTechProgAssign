Bohemian Haven - Ella


Nestled amidst the pristine, breezing peaks of Ella, located in the hills, our reort is a haven of warmth and opulence in the heart of the winter wonderland. Here, where the air is crisp and the scenery breathtaking, we invite you to experience a retreat unlike any other. Embrace the charm of the mountains in lavish comfort, where every guest is treated to a cocoon of luxury amid the frosty embrace of nature.


Alpine Observatory Deck
Imagine having access to a dedicated observatory deck where you can stargaze and marvel at the majesty of the night sky. Located high in the mountains, away from city lights, our Alpine Observatory Deck offers state-of-the-art telescopes, guided astronomy sessions, and comfortable seating. Whether you're an avid astronomer or simply want to appreciate the celestial wonders, this facility allows you to connect with the cosmos like never before.


Mountain Adventure Concierge
For thrill-seekers and nature enthusiasts, our Mountain Adventure Concierge is your gateway to unforgettable experiences. This facility provides expert guidance on local hiking trails, mountain biking routes, rock climbing excursions, and other outdoor adventures. You can also rent specialized gear and arrange guided tours, ensuring you make the most of your mountain retreat.


Elevated Spa with Panoramic Views
Our Elevated Spa offers a serene sanctuary where you can unwind while surrounded by breathtaking mountain vistas. Enjoy a range of signature treatments and therapies, from hot stone massages to Alpine herbal wraps. What sets this spa apart is its floor-to-ceiling windows that frame the stunning natural beauty outside, allowing you to relax in tranquility while staying connected to the rugged landscape.


These unique facilities not only enhance your stay in a mountain area but also provide opportunities for adventure, relaxation, and a deeper connection with the stunning natural surroundings.

Bohemian Haven - Nuwara Eliya


Welcome to Bohemian Haven - Nuwara Eliya, where luxury meets the crisp embrace of a cool mountain haven. Nestled in a region known for its picturesque landscapes and invigorating climate, our hotel offers a unique blend of warmth and sophistication. Here, you can immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature, explore the beauty of the surroundings, and savor the comforts of refined hospitality without the snow. Discover a retreat that combines the serenity of the mountains with the indulgence of a world-class resort.


Hot Springs Oasis
Experience the bliss of natural hot springs in our hotel's exclusive oasis. Nestled within the serene landscape, our hot springs offer a soothing escape from the chilly climate. Relax in the rejuvenating warmth of mineral-rich waters while taking in panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. This tranquil retreat is the perfect way to unwind and embrace the unique charm of the region.


Gourmet Fireside Dining
Delight in a culinary adventure like no other with our Gourmet Fireside Dining experience. Enjoy private, outdoor dining by a crackling bonfire, complete with cozy blankets and plush seating. Our talented chefs prepare a delectable feast, combining locally sourced ingredients with international flavors. It's a perfect way to savor delicious cuisine while staying warm and enjoying the crisp mountain air.


Aurora Viewing Lounge
Even in non-snowing areas, you can still witness the mesmerizing beauty of the night sky. Our hotel offers an Aurora Viewing Lounge equipped with high-powered telescopes and a dedicated guide to help you spot the Northern Lights when they grace the heavens. Immerse yourself in the celestial wonder of the region from the warmth and comfort of our lounge.


These unique facilities not only provide a luxurious experience but also allow guests to embrace the natural beauty and ambiance of the cold mountain area in a way that's both comfortable and captivating.

Bohemian Haven - Jaffna


Welcome to Bohemian Haven - Jaffna, where the rich tapestry of South Indian culture intertwines seamlessly with world-class luxury. Nestled in the heart of this vibrant region, our hotel offers a captivating blend of traditional charm and contemporary opulence. From the moment you arrive, you'll be immersed in the warm hospitality, exquisite cuisine, and cultural treasures of South India. Discover a haven where ancient traditions meet modern comfort, inviting you to explore, rejuvenate, and indulge in the essence of this culturally rich destination.


Saree Boutique and Draping Workshop
Immerse yourself in the elegance of South Indian attire with our on-site Saree Boutique and Draping Workshop. Guests can explore and purchase exquisite sarees, dhotis, and traditional garments. Additionally, our skilled attendants offer personalized lessons on how to drape these garments in the authentic South Indian style, allowing you to embrace the local culture in a meaningful way.


Culinary Heritage Tours and Cooking Classes
Indulge your taste buds and immerse yourself in the rich culinary traditions of South India with our Culinary Heritage Tours and Cooking Classes. Join guided tours of local markets to select fresh ingredients, spices, and herbs, then return to the hotel for hands-on cooking classes. Learn to prepare authentic South Indian dishes under the guidance of our expert chefs and savor the flavors of the region.


Silk Weaving Studio and Textile Museum
South India is renowned for its intricate silk weaving techniques and textiles. Our hotel features an in-house Silk Weaving Studio where guests can observe skilled artisans at work, creating beautiful silk fabrics and sarees. Additionally, our Textile Museum showcases a curated collection of historical and contemporary South Indian textiles, providing insight into the region's textile heritage.


These facilities not only offer a luxurious stay but also provide guests with immersive cultural experiences that allow them to appreciate the rich traditions, arts, and craftsmanship of South India.
