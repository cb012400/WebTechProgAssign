Locations You Can Choose to Host Your Special Day.


Oceanfront Terrace Wedding:


Say "I do" with the serene sounds of the ocean as your soundtrack on our Oceanfront Terrace. This picturesque setting offers panoramic views of the sparkling sea, making it an ideal spot for a romantic beachfront ceremony. The gentle sea breeze and the golden hues of the setting sun create a magical ambiance, perfect for exchanging vows. After the ceremony, your guests can enjoy a cocktail reception against the backdrop of the ocean waves, making this location a truly memorable choice for a seaside wedding.




Grand Ballroom Extravaganza:


For those seeking a more traditional and opulent setting, our Grand Ballroom is a timeless choice. With its high ceilings, crystal chandeliers, and elegant decor, it exudes luxury and sophistication. This spacious venue can accommodate large guest lists and can be customized to match your wedding theme and color palette. Whether you prefer a classic or contemporary look, the Grand Ballroom provides a versatile canvas for your vision. Our expert team will ensure every detail is meticulously planned, from the grand entrance to the last dance, making your reception an unforgettable event.




Rooftop Garden Romance:


Elevate your wedding experience to new heights with our Rooftop Garden. This hidden gem offers a lush and intimate setting for your special day. Surrounded by vibrant greenery and under a canopy of twinkling lights, it's a romantic oasis in the heart of the city. Perfect for smaller gatherings and intimate ceremonies, the Rooftop Garden provides a sense of seclusion and tranquility, making it an ideal escape from the hustle and bustle. Whether you're exchanging vows under the open sky or dancing beneath the stars, this rooftop retreat offers a unique and enchanting atmosphere for your wedding.
