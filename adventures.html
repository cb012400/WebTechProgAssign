Adventures by the Shore: Your Gateway to Oceanic Thrills


Experience the exhilaration of seaside exploration with our Adventures by the Shore program. Dive into a world of aquatic wonders, embark on island escapades, and relish the serenity of sunset cruises - all set against the stunning backdrop of our beachfront oasis.

At Bohemian Haven, we believe that every stay should be an unforgettable adventure. Our beachfront location provides the perfect backdrop for thrilling experiences that allow you to connect with the natural beauty of the sea. Whether you're seeking underwater wonders, island exploration, or sunset serenity, our curated adventures offer something for everyone. Explore our Adventures by the Shore program.


Island Excursions and Snorkeling Adventures:


Arrange guided island-hopping excursions to nearby islands, where guests can explore pristine beaches, lush jungles, and vibrant coral reefs. Snorkeling adventures are a popular highlight, allowing guests to discover the mesmerizing underwater world of colorful fish and coral formations. Expert guides ensure a safe and educational experience for guests of all skill levels.

Kayaking and Paddleboarding Expeditions:


Provide guests with the opportunity to embark on kayaking or paddleboarding adventures along the coast. These self-paced journeys allow individuals or groups to explore hidden coves, sea caves, and mangrove forests at their own pace. Guided tours are available for those seeking more structured experiences, while equipment rental is perfect for the independent traveler.




More Info

Sunset Sailing and Whale Watching:


Offer sunset sailing cruises where guests can relax on a luxurious catamaran or sailboat while enjoying the breathtaking view of the sun setting over the horizon. Dolphin-watching tours are also a popular adventure, providing the chance to observe these playful marine creatures in their natural habitat. Expert naturalists on board provide insights into the local marine ecosystem.
